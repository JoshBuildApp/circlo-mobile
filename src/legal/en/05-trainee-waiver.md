## 3. Acknowledgment of Inherent Risk — Read This Carefully

**Sports and physical training activities involve inherent risks.** Some of these risks cannot be eliminated by even the most experienced coach, the safest equipment, or the best venue. By participating, you knowingly and voluntarily accept these risks.

The following risks are **inherent to the activities offered through Circlo**:

### 3.1 General physical risks (apply to most or all sports)
- Muscle strains, sprains, and tears
- Bruises, abrasions, lacerations, and contusions
- Joint injuries (knees, shoulders, elbows, ankles, wrists, spine)
- Bone fractures, including stress fractures
- Concussion or head injury
- Cardiac events (heart attack, arrhythmia) — particularly in high-intensity sports
- Heat illness, dehydration, hyponatremia
- Hypothermia in cold environments
- Aggravation of pre-existing conditions
- Fatigue, dizziness, fainting
- Allergic reactions to environment or equipment

### 3.2 Sport-specific risks
- **Contact and combat sports** (boxing, MMA, martial arts, soccer, basketball, rugby): cuts, dental injury, eye injury, broken nose, concussion, internal bruising, joint dislocation
- **Water sports** (swimming): drowning, near-drowning, water-borne illness, shallow-water blackout
- **Equipment sports** (tennis, padel, CrossFit, fitness): equipment failure, weight-drop injury, impact injury from balls / racquets / paddles, rope burns
- **Outdoor / endurance sports** (running, soccer): traffic, uneven surfaces, weather exposure, slips, falls, animal encounters
- **High-intensity sports**: rhabdomyolysis, exercise-induced asthma, exercise-induced cardiac events

### 3.3 Environmental & equipment risks
- Slippery floors, wet surfaces, uneven ground
- Equipment defects or improper assembly
- Falling or projectile equipment
- Other participants (in group sessions or shared venues)
- Weather conditions
- Hazardous materials at the venue

### 3.4 Travel-related risks
- Risks of traveling to and from the session venue (which are entirely your responsibility)

You acknowledge that this list is **not exhaustive** and that other risks may exist that are not listed here.

### 6.1 Scope of the Release — What Is NOT Waived
**This Release does NOT cover, and you do NOT waive, claims for injury caused by:**

- Any other matter that, under Israeli law, **cannot lawfully be waived by contract** — including but not limited to consumer rights under the Consumer Protection Law, rights under the Standard Form Contracts Law, and rights of minors that cannot be waived even by a parent or guardian.

You and Circlo agree that this Release is intended to be enforced **only to the extent permitted by Israeli law** and not beyond.
