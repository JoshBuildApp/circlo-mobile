Circlo is a **two-sided marketplace** that connects sports trainees ("Trainees") with independent sports coaches ("Coaches") for the booking and delivery of in-person and online training sessions, and for related social, content, and community features.

### 2.1 Circlo Is a Marketplace, Not a Service Provider
**Circlo is an intermediary platform.** Circlo does not employ Coaches, does not deliver coaching services, does not supervise the content or quality of any session, and does not guarantee any specific outcome from your training. ## 6. Cancellation & Refund Policy

### 6.1 Trainee Cancellation
- **More than 24 hours before** the scheduled session start time: Trainee may cancel for a full refund, less the Stripe processing fee where non-refundable.
- **Within 24 hours of** the scheduled session start time: no refund, except where the Coach consents to reschedule or refund at their discretion.
- **Coach no-show or material non-delivery**: Trainee receives a full refund (including Stripe fees, where Circlo is able to recover them).

### 6.2 Coach Cancellation
A Coach who cancels less than 24 hours before a session must offer the Trainee either a full refund or a free rescheduling. Repeated late cancellations by a Coach may result in account suspension.

### 6.3 Force Majeure
Neither party is in breach for cancellation due to force majeure: severe weather, security situation, IDF call-up (miluim) on short notice, public health restrictions, infrastructure failure, or other circumstances beyond reasonable control. In a force majeure cancellation, Circlo will refund the Trainee in full.

### 6.4 Consumer Protection Law (חוק הגנת הצרכן) — Cooling-Off Disclosure
Israeli Consumer Protection Law generally provides a **14-day cooling-off period** for distance-sale services (extending to 4 months for users with disabilities, seniors aged 65+, and new immigrants resident in Israel less than 5 years).

**However**, under Section 14C(d)(2) of the Consumer Protection Law, the statutory cooling-off period **does not apply** to a service that is to be performed at a **specific scheduled date and time** and is to be consumed within **7 days** of that scheduled date. Sports coaching sessions booked through Circlo fall within this carve-out.

If the Coach offers a multi-session package (e.g. 10-session block), the cooling-off treatment may differ.]

The cancellation policy in Sections 6.1–6.3 above represents Circlo's contractual cancellation policy, which applies in addition to (and to the extent permitted by) any non-waivable statutory rights.

### 6.5 Refund Mechanics
Refunds are issued to the original payment method through Stripe and may take 5–14 business days to appear on the Trainee's statement, depending on the issuing bank.
