## 1. Overview

This policy explains when you can cancel a session, how refunds work, and what your rights are under Israeli law. It applies to all paid sessions booked through https://circloclub.com.

The full legal terms are in the [Terms of Service](https://circloclub.com/legal/terms) — Section 6 specifically.

---

## 2. Trainee Cancellation

### 2.1 More than 24 hours before the session
You may cancel for a **full refund** of the session price. The refund is issued to your original payment method, less any non-refundable Stripe processing fee where Circlo is unable to recover it.

### 2.2 Within 24 hours of the session
**No refund is owed.** You may, at the Coach's discretion, ask the Coach to reschedule or to issue a goodwill refund — but this is not guaranteed.

### 2.3 No-show by the Trainee
If you do not show up to a session you booked, no refund is owed.

---

## 3. Coach Cancellation

### 3.1 Coach cancels in advance
The Coach should give as much notice as possible. If the Coach cancels:
- **Trainee receives a full refund**, OR
- The Coach offers a free reschedule, AND the Trainee accepts.

### 3.2 Coach no-show
If the Coach does not show up to the session, the Trainee receives a **full refund** including, where Circlo can recover them, Stripe processing fees.

Repeated late cancellations or no-shows by a Coach may result in account suspension under the Coach Agreement.

---

## 4. Force Majeure

Neither the Trainee nor the Coach is in breach for cancellation due to circumstances beyond reasonable control. This includes:
- Severe weather (storm, snow, dangerous heat warning, lightning, flooding)
- Security situation (rocket fire, military operation, civil emergency)
- IDF reserve duty (miluim) call-up at short notice
- Public-health restrictions or quarantine
- Major venue closure or infrastructure failure
- Personal medical emergency (with reasonable evidence)

In a force-majeure cancellation, **Circlo will refund the Trainee in full**.

## 5. Your Statutory Rights — Cooling-Off Period

Israeli **Consumer Protection Law (חוק הגנת הצרכן תשמ"א-1981)** generally gives consumers a **14-day cooling-off period** for distance-purchased services, extending to **4 months** for users with disabilities, seniors aged 65+, and new immigrants resident in Israel less than 5 years.

**However**, under **Section 14C(d)(2)** of the Consumer Protection Law, this cooling-off period **does not apply** to a service that is to be performed at a **specific scheduled date and time** and is consumed within **7 days** of that date. **Sports coaching sessions booked through Circlo fall within this carve-out** — they are services performed at a specific scheduled time.

[VERIFY with counsel: this is the standard reading of Section 14C(d)(2). Confirm that Circlo's scheduled coaching sessions are correctly characterized. If a Coach offers a multi-session package (e.g. a 10-session block), the cooling-off treatment may differ — package purchases may retain the 14-day right.]

This means:
- For a **single scheduled session**: the 14-day cooling-off does not apply. The cancellation policy in Sections 2–4 above governs.
- For a **multi-session package** [VERIFY: only if Circlo offers packages]: the 14-day cooling-off may apply. Contact circlomanagement@circloclub.com.

The cancellation policy in this document is in addition to (and does not waive) any non-waivable statutory right you may have.

## 6. Refund Mechanics

### 6.1 Method
Refunds are issued to your original payment method via Stripe.

### 6.2 Timeline
Refunds typically appear on your statement within **5–14 business days** of approval, depending on your card issuer's processing time. Circlo and Coach do not control bank-side processing.

### 6.3 Confirmation
You will receive an email confirmation when a refund is initiated.

### 6.4 Currency
Refunds are issued in the currency of the original payment (₪ for Israeli accounts).

### 6.5 Refund Receipts / Credit Notes (added in v1.1)
For Trainees who require a tax credit-note (חשבונית זיכוי) — typically Coaches who paid through a business account or Trainees claiming the session as a business expense — Circlo can issue a credit-note via its invoicing pipeline (Green Invoice / Morning or equivalent). Request via circlomanagement@circloclub.com with the subject line "Credit Note — booking #[ID]". Credit notes will be issued within **7 business days** and emailed in PDF.

## 7. Disputed Charges (Chargebacks)

If you believe you have been incorrectly charged, please contact **circlomanagement@circloclub.com** before initiating a chargeback with your bank. Most disputes can be resolved more quickly through us than through the bank's chargeback process.

If you initiate a chargeback that is later determined to be unjustified, Circlo reserves the right to suspend your account.
