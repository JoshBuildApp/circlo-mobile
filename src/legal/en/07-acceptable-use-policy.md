## 1. Who This Applies To

This Acceptable Use Policy ("**Policy**") applies to **everyone who uses the Circlo platform** — Trainees, Coaches, admins, parents/guardians signing on behalf of minors, visitors, and anyone interacting with the Platform via API or otherwise.

By using Circlo, you agree to follow this Policy. Violation may result in content removal, account suspension, account termination, refusal of refunds for ongoing bookings, financial liability for damage caused, and (in serious cases) referral to Israeli law enforcement.

---

## 2. Prohibited Behavior

You **may not** use Circlo to:

### 2.1 Harm other people
- Harass, bully, threaten, intimidate, dox, or stalk any user
- Send unsolicited romantic, sexual, or commercial messages
- Engage in any sexual conduct involving a minor — **ever, under any circumstances**
- Discriminate against any user on the basis of race, religion, gender, sexual orientation, age, disability, national origin, or any other protected characteristic
- Solicit, encourage, or facilitate self-harm
- Make credible threats of violence

### 2.2 Misrepresent yourself or others
- Use a false name, false age, or false credentials
- Impersonate another person, a Coach, a celebrity, or Circlo staff
- Misrepresent your qualifications, certifications, licenses, or insurance
- Create multiple accounts to evade a suspension or to manipulate ratings

### 2.3 Misuse the Platform
- **Off-platform booking circumvention** — using the Platform to identify or initially connect with another user, then taking the booking off-platform to avoid Circlo's commission
- Attempt to scrape, reverse engineer, or extract Platform data using bots, crawlers, or automation that is not explicitly authorized
- Probe, scan, or test the vulnerability of the Platform without prior written permission from Circlo
- Bypass authentication, rate limits, geographic restrictions, or any other technical control
- Interfere with the operation of the Platform — including DDoS, spam, fake reviews, fake bookings, or fake account creation
- Abuse refund requests, chargebacks, or dispute mechanisms in bad faith

### 2.4 Upload illegal or harmful content
- Content that infringes copyright, trademark, patent, trade secret, or any other IP right
- Content that violates a person's right to privacy or publicity, including unauthorized photos or videos of identifiable people
- Defamatory, libelous, or knowingly false content about another person
- Content that depicts or promotes violence, hate speech, terrorism, or incitement
- Sexual content involving minors — **strictly prohibited**; will be reported to Israeli law enforcement
- Adult sexual content — Circlo is not a platform for adult content
- Content that promotes illegal drugs, illegal weapons, or other illegal goods/services
- Content containing personal data of third parties without their consent
- Content containing malware, viruses, or any code intended to harm

### 2.5 Use Circlo for illegal purposes
- Money laundering, tax evasion, fraud
- Coaching of activities that are illegal in Israel
- Misuse of payment processing for non-coaching transactions
- Sanctioned-party transactions (per Israeli sanctions law and any applicable international sanctions)

### 2.6 Operate as something you're not
- Operate as a coaching agency or coach-of-record without registering as a Coach and accepting the Coach Agreement
- Sell access to your account
- Resell coaching sessions for a markup outside the Platform's pricing controls
- Use the Trainee account type to deliver paid coaching services

## 3. Sport-Specific Conduct Standards

In addition to the general rules above, Coaches must:

- Follow the safety standards reasonable for their sport
- Use appropriate safety equipment (mats, gloves, helmets, lifeguard certification, defibrillator access where required)
- Not coach a sport for which they lack the required license or certification under Israeli law (e.g. swimming-related coaching where lifeguard certification is required, certain combat sports requiring federation accreditation)
- Refuse to coach a Trainee who is visibly intoxicated, injured beyond safe participation, or whose disclosed medical condition makes the session unsafe
- Conduct sessions at safe, reasonable, professional venues — not in inappropriate private locations

Trainees must:
- Follow the Coach's instructions during sessions
- Not attempt techniques beyond their physical or skill capacity
- Disclose any medical condition relevant to the session (per the Trainee Waiver §4)
- Not bring children, pets, or unauthorized observers to a session unless agreed with the Coach
- Treat the venue and equipment with reasonable care

## 4. Reporting Violations & Security Incidents

### 4.1 Security Incident Reporting (added in v1.1)
If you believe you have discovered a **security vulnerability, data breach, account compromise, or fraudulent activity** affecting Circlo or another user, please report it as soon as possible to **circlomanagement@circloclub.com** with the subject line "Security Incident — [brief]". For coordinated disclosure of a vulnerability, please give Circlo a reasonable opportunity (target 30 days) to remediate before public disclosure.

Where applicable, Circlo aligns its incident-handling practice with **INCD (Maarach HaSyber)** general guidance for small online businesses, including: prompt containment, written timeline of the incident, notification to affected users where the incident may cause significant harm (per Section 13 of the Privacy Policy), and good-faith cooperation with law enforcement when criminal conduct is involved. Circlo will not pursue legal action against good-faith security researchers who follow coordinated-disclosure norms.

### 4.2 General Violation Reporting

If you witness a violation of this Policy:

- **Use the in-app "Report" button** on any profile, message, video, post, or chat
- **Email** circlomanagement@circloclub.com with the subject "Report — [type]"
- **In an emergency** affecting personal safety, contact **Israeli police (100)** or **Magen David Adom (101)** before or in addition to reporting to Circlo

For reports involving suspected criminal conduct (sexual offenses, violence, fraud, threats), Circlo will cooperate with Israeli law enforcement and may itself report to authorities, with or without notice to the reported user.

## 5. Circlo's Enforcement Actions

Depending on severity and context, Circlo may take any of the following actions, with or without prior notice:

| Action | Triggers |
|---|---|
| Warning | Minor first-time violations |
| Content removal | Specific content violates this Policy |
| Temporary suspension (24h–30 days) | Repeated minor violations, single moderate violation |
| Account termination | Single severe violation, repeated moderate violations after warning |
| Forfeiture of pending payouts | Where the violation caused harm to other users (e.g. fraud) |
| Financial liability | Where the violation caused Circlo or another user direct, quantifiable damage |
| Referral to law enforcement | Suspected criminal conduct, especially involving minors |
| Civil claim | At Circlo's discretion, for material breach |

Circlo will, where reasonable, give the affected user notice and an opportunity to respond. In emergency or law-enforcement-related cases, Circlo may act without notice.

## 6. Appeals

If your account is suspended or terminated, you may appeal by emailing **circlomanagement@circloclub.com** with subject "Appeal — [your user ID]" within **14 days** of the action. Circlo will review and respond within **14 business days**. Appeal decisions are final but do not affect any non-waivable statutory rights you may have under Israeli law.
